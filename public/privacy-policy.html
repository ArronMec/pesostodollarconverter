Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Pesos to Dollar Converter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our currency converter application ("App").

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

None. Pesos to Dollar Converter does not require user accounts, registration, or any personal information to function.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Exchange Rate Data: The App caches current and historical USD/MXN exchange rates locally on your device to provide offline functionality and improve performance.
  • App Preferences: The App stores your onboarding completion status and app preferences locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults).
  • Purchase Information: Subscription purchase data is handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information, billing address, or other financial details.

How We Use Information

  • Exchange Rate Conversion: To provide real-time and historical currency conversion between US Dollars (USD) and Mexican Pesos (MXN).
  • App Functionality: To remember your preferences and provide a personalized experience.
  • Subscription Management: To verify your premium subscription status and unlock premium features.

Third-Party Services

Exchange Rate APIs

Pesos to Dollar Converter retrieves exchange rate data from the following third-party services:

  • open.er-api.com: Provides current exchange rates. Requests are anonymous and do not include any user identifiers.
  • api.frankfurter.app: Provides historical exchange rate data from the European Central Bank. Requests are anonymous and keyless.

Both services are free, public APIs that do not require user accounts or transmit personal information. We cache responses to minimize API requests and ensure offline functionality.

Apple Services

  • StoreKit: All subscription purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to purchase transactions: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Data Storage

All data collected by Pesos to Dollar Converter is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your information, and we do not transmit your data to third parties except as necessary to fetch exchange rate data (which is done anonymously).

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect data stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Pesos to Dollar Converter is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data stored by the App through your device's settings.
  • Deletion: You can delete the App at any time, which will remove all locally stored data.
  • Subscription Cancellation: You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID account settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing or via the support contact information provided in the App.

Apple App Store Requirements
